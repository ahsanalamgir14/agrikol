Milestones

Creative Sustainability Group is under a process of constant innovation and extention in terms of its product line by introducing vast range of products. Below is the list of milestone achieved so far and still more to come.

  • 1993: Inception of parent company Creative Engineering Pvt. Ltd. for manufacturing of Electrical Line Hardware and a wide variety of related products.
  • 2003: New sister company, Vision Engineering Pvt. Ltd. was founded for production of PCC Poles, Spun Poles and Steel Structures etc.
  • 2005: ISO 9001:2000 Certified.
  • 2006: Another sister concern, Creative Electronics Pvt. Ltd. was founded for production of Energy Meters and other accessories.
  • 2010: Another state of the art unit for GRP/FRP pipe manufacturing and other composite products was established.
  • 2012: A novel industrial scale Biogas-Bioenergy facility had been installed and become operational.
  • 2015: ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management Standard) and ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management Standard) Certifications achieved.
  • 2017: Another large-commercial scale Biogas cum Biofertilizer processing facility was established.
  • 2023: A new venture organization, Creative Sustanability Solutions has been established for provision of services regarding safeguarding the environmental, economic, and social sustainability.

About

The organization was incepted in 1993 with a Vision to provide the valued customers a vast and comprehensive range of Creative, Innovative and Quality Electrical, Metallurgical and FRP/GRP products, components and customized solutions.
